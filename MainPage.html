About Us

DOG

A four legged creature which symbolize as the faithful and loyal friend of man. Whatever the situation they are facing, nothing will induce them to leave their master. They obey all the orders of their owner very sincerely. And using their acute sense, being alert all time whether day or night to offer its services to the owner. But sometimes, they also do silly things to bring joy to their master or others.

\ Purpose of creating this website

Beside this is part of the project which attach great importance to this lecture,
we all appreciate doggy as a pet, which let us release from the daily stress or anxiety by watching their dumb action with their cute face, no matter on the street or in the Internet. So we want to use this opportunity to share this short moment of happiness to everyone of you.

Thus, we choose the most common dog breeds as our sources to introduce most of the features and characteristic of them. Using a simple and minimal design style to interpret our concept, avoid using complex structure to create unnecessary troubles. Wish all of you can earn something while browsing.
