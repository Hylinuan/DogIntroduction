CHIHUAHUA

from MEXICO

The Chihuahua is the smallest breed of dog and is named after the state of Chihuahua in Mexico. Chihuahuas come in a wide variety of colors, and two coat lengths.

Chihuahuas are the smallest breed recognized by some kennel clubs. There are two varieties of Chihuahua – the Smooth Coat (shorthaired) and the Long Coat (longhaired).

Both the Smooth and the Long Coats have their special attractions and are equally easy to keep clean and well groomed.
